# Operators

### CS-104 · Week 2

---

## Operators We Use Often

- Arithmetic: `+`, `-`, `*`, `/`, `%`
- Comparison: `==`, `!=`, `<`, `>`, `<=`, `>=`
- Logical: `and`, `or`, `not`

---

## Expressions

- Python evaluates expressions to produce values.
- Parentheses help make intent clear.
- Predict first, then run.

---

## Common Mistakes

- Confusing `=` with `==`
- Forgetting operator precedence
- Mixing values without checking their types

---

## Good Practice

- Break complex expressions into smaller steps.
- Store intermediate results in variables.
- Test examples with known answers.
