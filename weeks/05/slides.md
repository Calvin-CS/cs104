# Loops

### CS-104 · Week 5

---

## Why Loops?

- Loops repeat work without copying code.
- They make patterns easier to express.
- They reduce mistakes from manual repetition.

---

## `for` Loops

- Best when you know what you are looping over.
- Common with `range(...)` and collections.

---

## `while` Loops

- Best when repetition depends on a condition.
- Make sure something changes inside the loop.

---

## Watch For

- Off-by-one errors
- Infinite loops
- Updating the wrong variable
