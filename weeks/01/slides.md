# Introduction

### CS-104 · Week 1

---

## What Is Programming?

- Programming means writing exact instructions for a computer.
- Computers do not guess what you meant.
- Small mistakes can change the result completely.

---

## Why Learn It?

- Solve problems step by step
- Automate repetitive work
- Build confidence with technology
- Practice precise thinking

---

## Python in This Course

- Beginner-friendly syntax
- Strong support for learning and debugging
- Widely used beyond this class

## Course Structure

- Lots of small practice
